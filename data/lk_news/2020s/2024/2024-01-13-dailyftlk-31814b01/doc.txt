Japanese Finance Minister strengthens bilateral ties with SL

Japan’s Finance Minister Suzuki Shunichi met with Speaker Mahinda Yapa Abeywardena at the Parliament complex yesterday.

The Secretary General of the Parliament Kushani Rohanadeera and the Japanese Ambassador to Sri Lanka Mizukoshi Hideaki also attended the meeting.

Minister Shunichi emphasised the enduring friendship between Japan and Sri Lanka. He conveyed Japan’s commitment to providing support for Sri Lanka’s economic stabilisation, signifying the collaborative efforts to strengthen economic ties between the two countries.

The meeting also delved into the ongoing developments in the friendship and potential avenues for future cooperation.

He conveyed his optimism for sustained collaboration, expressing the hope that Japan would remain a key partner in the nation’s future endeavours and added that he hopes for Japan’s support in the future.
