Sri Lanka rupee closes stronger at 300.60/75, bond yields up further

ECONOMYNEXT – Sri Lanka’s rupee closed stronger at 300.60/75 to the US dollar on Tuesday, from 301.50/302.00 on Monday, dealers said, while bond yields were up.

A bond maturing on 15.12.2026 closed at 11.30/50 percent, down from 11.30/55 percent.

A bond maturing on 01.07.2028 closed at 13.00/20 percent, up from 12.90/13.20 percent.

A bond maturing on 18.10.2030 closed at 13.30/60 percent. (Colombo/Sep10/2024)
