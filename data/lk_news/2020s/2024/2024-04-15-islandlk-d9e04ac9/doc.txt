Surado Campus holds grand graduation ceremony 2024

Surado Campus of Business Management, the leading, privately-led education institution that offers professional qualifications for semi-skilled and skilled Sri Lankans in caregiving, pharmacology, and other related fields in the healthcare industry, recently celebrated the culmination of the 2023 academic year with a grand graduation ceremony. The prestigious event took place at the BMICH Lotus Hall, marking a significant milestone for the institute and its graduating students.

Over 200 remarkable students, who have shown unwavering dedication and excellence throughout their studies, were proudly awarded their degrees. The prestigious occasion was graced by the presence of Sobitha Rajakaruna Judge of the Court of Appeal.
