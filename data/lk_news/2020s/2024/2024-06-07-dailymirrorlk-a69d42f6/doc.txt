32 train journeys cancelled due to the railway strike

Colombo, June 07 (Daily Mirror)- More than 30 train services have been cancelled today due to the strike launched by the Sri Lanka Railways Locomotive Operating Engineersâ€™ Union, since midnight yesterday, the Control Rooms sources said.
