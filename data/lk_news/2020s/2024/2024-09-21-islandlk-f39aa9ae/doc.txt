Turnover stood at Rs 1.3 billion with a crossing. The crossing was reported in HNB, which crossed 1.14 million shares to the tune of Rs 189 million; its shares traded at Rs 109.

In the retail market, companies that mainly contributed the turnover were; HNB Rs 271 million (1.6 million shares traded), Commercial Bank Rs 152 million (1.7 million shares traded), Sampath Bank Rs 72.9 million (1 million shares traded), NTB Rs 50.1 million (434,009 shares traded), Hemas Holdings Rs.47 million (651,000 shares traded), LOLC Finance Rs 38.1 million (6.9 million shares traded) and Browns Investment Rs 38.1 million (6.9 million shares traded). During the day 43 million share volumes changed hands in 11600 transactions.
