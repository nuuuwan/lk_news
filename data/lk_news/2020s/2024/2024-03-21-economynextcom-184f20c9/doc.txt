Sri Lanka fertiliser subsidy for Yala season to cost Rs7.5 bn

ECONOMYNEXT – Sri Lanka is to allocate 7.5 billion rupees for fertiliser subsidies this yala season for paddy cultivation or other agricultural needs, Minister of Agriculture Mahinda Amaraweera has said.

“The government will allocate 7,500 million rupees to facilitate farmers to buy fertilizer required for paddy cultivation in the 2024 yala season, or to use for other agricultural needs,” Minister of Agriculture and Plantation Industry Mahinda Amaraweera was quoted in a statement by the Department of Government Information.

The Department of Agrarian Development has made arrangements to transfer 15,000 rupees per hectare to the farmers’ accounts, altogether subsidising the purchase of fertiliser for 500,000 hectares of paddy fields, the statement said.

The government had spent 11.5 billion rupees for subsidising fertiliser in 2023.

The Cabinet approved the proposal for subsidies made by Minister Amaraweera last week. (Colombo/Mar21/2024)
