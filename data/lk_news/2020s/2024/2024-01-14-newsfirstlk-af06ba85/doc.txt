Keheliya Rambukwella summoned for more drug scandal interrogation

Former Health Minister Keheliya Rambukwella will be called in for further questioning regarding the ongoing drug scandal investigation.

The Criminal Investigation Department (CID) announced this decision after making progress in their investigations involving files related to Ministry's Additional Secretary Dr. Saman Ratnayake.

Both the CID and the Attorney General's Department are actively involved in uncovering the truth.

Rambukwella's additional statement will be sought once the relevant investigations are complete, focusing on the questionable immunoglobulin drug transaction.
