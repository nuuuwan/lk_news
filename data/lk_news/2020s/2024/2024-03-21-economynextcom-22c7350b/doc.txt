Sri Lanka’s Cargills launches EV charging facility at supermarket chain

ECONOMYNEXT – Cargills (Ceylon) in collaboration with the United States Agency for International Development (USAID) launched its ‘Charge while you shop’ initiative to offer electric vehicle charging stations at multiple Cargills retail locations today.

The move aims to support of Sri Lanka’s transition to electric mobility, Minister of Power and Energy Kanchana Wijesekera said at the launch this morning at the Baudhaloka Mawatha Cargills outlet.

US Ambassador to Sri Lanka Julie Chung was present at the launch. (Colombo/Mar21)
