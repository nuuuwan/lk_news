Puvendran Gajendra appointed Chairman of Sanasa Life Insurance PLC

Sanasa Life Insurance PLC has appointed Puvendran Gajendra as their new Chairman. Gajendra has been a board member and served as an independent Director since 2019. He is a Fellow Member of the Institute of Chartered Accountants of Sri Lanka (FCA), an Associate Member of the Association of Chartered Certified Accountants (ACCA UK), and a Fellow Member of the Institute of Certified Management Accountants of Sri Lanka (FCMA). Additionally, he holds the title of Fellow Member of the Institute of Chartered Professional Managers of Sri Lanka (FCPM) and possesses a Postgraduate Diploma in Business Administration from Sheffield Hallam University.

Gajendra began his career two decades ago as an audit trainee at Kanesalingam & Co – Chartered Accountants, where he currently serves as managing partner alongside his role as a board member of Sanasa Life Insurance PLC. In discussing future challenges, Gajendra emphasizes the importance of investing in value-added technological innovations such as Artificial Intelligence for data mining and accurate financial predictions.

Commenting on Sanasa Life Insurance’s mission, Gajendra stated, “Sanasa Life Insurance’s origins were rooted in assisting small-scale farming communities in overcoming financial challenges through cooperative-based institutions. We continue to uphold this core ethic by reaching out to these communities and delivering steadfast solutions through life insurance.
