OMAK re-launches website for greater customer experience

OMAK Technologies, a leader in restaurant and retail management solutions, is thrilled to announce the official launch of its redesigned website, https://omak.co. The new website boasts a modern design, intuitive navigation, and a wealth of information about the company’s cutting-edge products and services

OMAK Technologies is inviting visitors to explore it’s new website.  The new website has also been designed to offer the ultimate user-friendly experience with improved navigation and functionality. Every element of this visually captivating design, has been carefully curated to showcase the essence of the brand while providing an aesthetically pleasing and intuitive browsing experience.

The website is fully responsive across all devices and ensures a consistent and engaging experience whether accessed from a desktop, tablet, or smartphone. This responsiveness not only speaks to the Company’s dedication to user satisfaction but also aligns with its commitment to reaching and serving a broad audience.

Omak Technologies, a cloud-based POS, is one of the fastest growing restaurant and retail management solutions provider operating a SaaS (Software as a Service) model. The Company has been committed to providing industry-leading solutions for over a decade and its solutions are currently deployed at a number of F&B outlets in Sri Lanka and the region. OMAK is the 1st POS provider to integrate with PICK ME Foods and UBER EATS in Sri Lanka.
