Aitken Spence Printing celebrates wins at Asian Packaging Excellence Awards, Vietnam and Asian Print Awards, Indonesia

Deputy General Manager Nalindra Fonseka receiving the award at the Asian Print Awards 2024 ceremony held in Indonesia

In an impressive display of excellence, Aitken Spence Printing received two ‘Bronze’ awards in the Digital Printing category at two prestigious industry events held in Vietnam and Indonesia. Notably, this recognition is an achievement among other competitors in the South-East Asian region.

The company’s cutting-edge print quality and exceptional craftsmanship were recognised at the Asian Packaging Excellence Awards (APEA) that was recently held in Ho Chi Minh, Vietnam; followed closely by their success on the Asian Print Awards (APA) in Jakarta, Indonesia that was also held in November 2024. These consecutive wins not only highlight the company’s unwavering commitment to industry standards but also underscore their leadership in the digital printing sector. The awards serve as a powerful testament to the company’s dedication to delivering superior print quality, innovation, and precision, setting them apart as a trailblazer in the highly competitive Asian printing landscape. “These awards are a testament to the hard work, dedication, and creativity of our entire team,” commented Aitken Spence Printing Director/CEO Deshantha Silva. “We are incredibly proud to be recognised for our excellence at this regional platform for both our printing and packaging services. These awards further encourage us to continue setting higher boundaries for innovation, quality, and sustainability in the printing and packaging industry.”
