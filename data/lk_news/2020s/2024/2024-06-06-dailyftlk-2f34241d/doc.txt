E. B. Creasy Solar energises 9th Annual Construction Power and Energy Expo 2024

E.B. Creasy Solar Sales Manager Eksath De Alwis hands over the Platinum Sponsorship cheque to Lanka Exhibition and Conference Services Managing Director Arjun Dharmadasa. LECS CEO Aasim Mukthar (right) and Project Manager Chamara Sooriyaarachchi are also present

E. B. Creasy Solar, has announced its Platinum Sponsorship of the Construction Power and Energy Expo 2024, Sri Lanka’s premier event dedicated to interior products, construction, power, and energy.

As a long-standing advocate of sustainable development, E. B. Creasy Solar said it is delighted to power this prestigious expo making its return for the 9th consecutive year. Scheduled to take place from 5 to 7 July at the Bandaranaike Memorial International Conference Hall (BMICH), Colombo, the exhibition will serve as a vital platform for industry professionals to showcase cutting-edge trends, technologies, and solutions.

By powering this coveted event, organised by the Lanka Exhibition and Conference Services (LECS), E. B. Creasy Solar demonstrates its unwavering commitment to advancing Sri Lanka’s growth and progress. Through its extensive expertise in the solar energy industry spanning over 20 years, E. B. Creasy Solar ensures that every project is executed with precision and dedication. The company’s strict procurement and supply chain practices reflect its loyalty to suppliers and principals, emphasising its dedication to customer satisfaction and long-term reliability in the Sri Lankan market.

E. B. Creasy Solar Chief Operating Officer Isuru Lekamge said: “The Construction and Power Expo 2024 is poised to build on the success of previous years in a significant way. At E .B. Creasy Solar, we eagerly anticipate empowering this exhibition which serves as a beacon of innovation and progress. As a longstanding advocate for sustainable energy solutions, we are excited to contribute to an event that explores new and promising technologies across various sectors. Together, we can pave the way for brighter and more sustainable futures, reflecting the core values and commitment to excellence that define E. B. Creasy Solar.”

As a proponent of sustainability and renewable energy initiatives, E. B. Creasy Solar will play a pivotal role in the Construction Power and Energy Expo 2024. The company’s renewable energy division focuses on Solar Grid Tied, Solar Irrigation and Solar Hybrid Solutions, aimed at improving lives in both domestic and industrial markets by providing clean, sustainable energy. Through strategic implementation of solar grid-tied systems, E. B. Creasy & Co. PLC has installed over 2.5MW on their own rooftop facilities and has been actively involved in multiple large-scale Industrial and commercial Renewable Energy Projects.

E. B. Creasy Solar is thrilled to support the Construction Power and Energy Expo 2024, demonstrating its dedication to fostering innovation and sustainability in Sri Lanka’s energy landscape. Through this collaboration, the company reaffirms its commitment to driving positive change and shaping a brighter future for generations to come.
