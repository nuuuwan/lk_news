Dreamron College celebrates 15th graduation, certifies 900+ students

Dreamron College of Art & Beauty recently held its 15th Graduation Ceremony at the BMICH in Colombo, where over 900 students received certificates for completing three key programs: NVQ Level 4 Beautician & Hairdresser, Diploma in Hairdressing & Beauty Culture, and NTT Haircutting. The college, a registered institution under the Tertiary & Vocational Education Commission (TVEC) and a partner of the National Apprentice and Industrial Training Authority (NAITA), has been a leader in beauty education for 17 years. With 10 branches across the country, Dreamron provides accessible, government-recognized programs, equipping graduates for both local and international career opportunities.
