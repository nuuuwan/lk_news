Sri Lanka to accept internationally recognised medical degrees

ECONOMYNEXT – Sri Lanka will change its medical ordinance to accept degrees awarded by internationally recognized universities, a government statement said.

Sri Lanka had appointed a Committee to prepare a national policy ‘to suit current needs’ and recognize internationally accepted medical degrees previously.

Based on its recommendations, amendments have been prepared for the medical ordinance.

The Attorney General has now approved the draft bill prepared by the Legal Draftsman to amend the Medical Ordinance.

Cabinet has approved the changes, which will be Gazetted and presented to Parliament. (Colombo/May7/2024)
