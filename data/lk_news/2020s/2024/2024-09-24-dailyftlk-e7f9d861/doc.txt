CA Sri Lanka congratulates new President of Sri Lanka

The Institute of Chartered Accountants of Sri Lanka would like to congratulate and extend our best wishes to the 9th Executive President of Sri Lanka, Hon. Anura Kumara Dissanayake.

As a national accounting body promoting high standards of accountability, transparency and governance, we look forward to supporting you in your endeavours  to build a  united and economically strong Sri Lanka that will lead to sustainable economic prosperity.

We would also like to extend our gratitude to the outgoing President, Hon. Ranil Wickremesinghe who rose to the challenge of keeping a nation rife with economic challenges afloat and steering us to where we are today.
