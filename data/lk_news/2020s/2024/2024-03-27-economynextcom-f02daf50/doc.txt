Sri Lanka Treasury bill yields fall across maturities

ECONOMYNEXT – Sri Lanka’s Treasury bill yields fell across maturities at Wednesday’s auction, data from the state debt office showed.

The 3-month Treasury bill yield fell 16 basis points to 10.07 percent with 20 billion rupees offered at 30.35 billion sold.

The 6-month yield fell 12 basis points to 10.23 percent with 30 billion rupees offered and 42 billion sold.

The 12-month yield fell 10 basis points to 10.28 percent with 20 billion rupees offered and 7.2 billion sold.

This week the central bank cut its policy rate 50 basis points. (Colombo/Mar28/2024)
