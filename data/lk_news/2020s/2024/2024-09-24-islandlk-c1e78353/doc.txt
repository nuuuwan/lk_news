Rotary Club of Colombo Mid Town celebrates 50 years with a vision for community impact

On August 23, 2024, the Rotary Club of Colombo Mid Town commemorated a remarkable milestone with its 50th installation ceremony, held at Elevate by Jetwing. The event, which saw over 90 attendees, celebrated the club’s five decades of unwavering dedication to community service.

The ceremony was honored by the presence of Rtn. Sushena Ranathunga, District Governor of District 3220, who lauded the club’s impressive achievements throughout its history. In his address, he urged members to maintain their steadfast commitment to making a positive difference in society.

A key moment of the evening was the installation of Rasini Bandara as the club’s 50th president. Rasini, a distinguished professional with expertise in psychology, public speaking, and personality development, steps into her new role with extensive experience. Her current roles as an International Wellbeing Consultant for the International Transport Federation and as a non-executive director at Mind Heals private limited position her well to lead the club’s upcoming initiatives.

As the Rotary Club of Colombo Mid Town embarks on its next chapter under President Rasini Bandara’s leadership, it reaffirms its dedication to community betterment. The club has an array of impactful projects planned for the calendar year, reflecting its ongoing commitment to building and supporting the community. The 50th installation ceremony not only honored the club’s rich legacy but also set the stage for a future filled with continued service and positive change.
