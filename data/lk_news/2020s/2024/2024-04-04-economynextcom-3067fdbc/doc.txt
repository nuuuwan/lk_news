Sri Lanka rupee opens at 299.85/95 to the US dollar

ECONOMYNEXT – Sri Lanka’s rupee opened at 299.85/95 to the US dollar in the spot forex market on Thursday, from 299.80/95 on Wednesday, dealers said, while bond yields were up.

A bond maturing on 15.12.2026 was quoted stable at 11.35/45 percent.

A bond maturing on 15.09.2027 was quoted at 11.95/12.05 percent from 11.90/12.00 percent.

A bond maturing on 15.12.2028 was quoted at 12.20/25 percent from 12.15/20 percent.

The Colombo Stock Exchange opened up: The All Share Price Index was up 0.48 percent at 11,681, and the S&P SL20 was up 0.68 percent at 3,416. (Colombo/Apr4/2024)
