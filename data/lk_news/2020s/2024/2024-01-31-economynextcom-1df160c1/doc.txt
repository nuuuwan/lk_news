Shasheendra Rajapaksa Sri Lanka’s new State Min for Irrigation and Water Resources

ECONOMYNEXT – Shasheendra Rajapaksa was appointed as Sri Lanka’s State Minister for Irrigation and Water Resources Management on Wednesday.

“Shasheendra Rajapaksa was sworn in as the State Minister for Irrigation and Water Resources Management before President Ranil Wickremesinghe at the Presidential Secretariat,” the president’s media division said.

The portfolio was left vacant after the death of parliamentarian Sanath Nishantha in a road accident last week.  (Colombo/Jan31/2024).
