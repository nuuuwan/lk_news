Pettigo, India’s Jio among investors interested in Sri Lanka Telecom

ECONOMYNEXT – Three investors have expressed interest in the sale of Sri Lanka Telecom shares, the island’s State-Owned Enterprises Restructuring Unit said.

They are: Jio Platforms Ltd, Gujrat, India, Gortune International Investment Holding Ltd, submitted by Capital Alliance Limited, and Pettigo Comercio International LDA submitted by NDB Investment Bank Ltd.

Jio Platforms is a unit of Reliance Industries, and Pettigo is part of the Lycamobile group.

The proposals will be evaluated under Special Guidelines on Divestiture of State-Owned Enterprises approved by the Cabinet of Ministers in July 2023.

The International Finance Corporation is financial advisors to the sale. (Colombo/Jan12/2023)
