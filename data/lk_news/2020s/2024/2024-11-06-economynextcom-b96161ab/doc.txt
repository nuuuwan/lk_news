Sri Lanka appoints three new treasury officials

ECONOMYNEXT – Sri Lanka’s Cabinet of Ministers has appointed R M S P S Bandara as the Director General of the External Resources Department of the Treasury, and appointed two other officers, Spokesman Vijitha Herath said.

Bandara served as the Additional Director General of the Department of External Resources.

S S Mudalige will be appointed to the post of Director General of the Department of Project Management and Monitoring.

He served as the Director General of the Ministry of Transport, Highways, Ports and Civil Aviation.

He will retain his position as the acting General Manager of Sri Lanka Railways.

Udeni Udugahapaththuwa will be appointed as Acting Director General of the proposed Department of Public Debt Management.

She is currently a Director of the External Resources Department. (Colombo/Nov6/2024)
