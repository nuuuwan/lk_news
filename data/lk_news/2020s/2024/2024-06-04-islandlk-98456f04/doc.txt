Dialog named Sri Lanka’s Most Loved Service and Telco Brand by LMD Brands Annual

Dialog Axiata PLC, Sri Lanka’s #1 connectivity provider, was named the Most Loved Service Brand and the Most Loved Telecommunications Brand in LMD’s Brands Annual study, reinforcing its longstanding position as the market leader.

This prestigious recognition is testament to the company’s steadfast commitment to delivering unparalleled network quality and exceptional customer value, as well as its unrelenting efforts towards fostering digital inclusion and pioneering technological innovation in the telecommunications sector. Aligned with its purpose of “empowering and enriching Sri Lankan lives and enterprises,” the company is also dedicated to uplifting the communities it serves, helping unlock the country’s full potential through inclusive solutions and digital innovation, advancing Sri Lanka’s journey towards becoming a digital nation.
