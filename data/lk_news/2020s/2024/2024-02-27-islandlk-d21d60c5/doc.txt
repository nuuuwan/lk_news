SLT-MOBITEL ‘One Shot ULTRA’ offers ultimate unlimited voice and data experience to mobile users

SLT-MOBITEL has unveiled the ultimate ‘One Shot ULTRA’ designed to revolutionize users’ communication experience with unparalleled benefits.

With SLT-MOBITEL’s unmatched network performance, One Shot ULTRA brings a significant upgrade to mobile internet in Sri Lanka, providing users with unlimited voice calls to any network and uninterrupted access to data. This ensures SLT-MOBITEL Mobile users experience seamless connectivity, allowing for uninterrupted data usage across any application without any limitations, coupled with added convenience. With One Shot ULTRA, SLT-MOBITEL has introduced a new era of connectivity, reshaping the landscape of the unlimited voice and data spaces.

One Shot ULTRA is currently available in 7-day, 30-day, 60-day, 90-day, and 180-day validity options, starting from just LKR 488 for prepaid connections. Priced at LKR 1,989, the 30-day pack is available for both post and prepaid connections. More product information can be obtained by visiting https://www.mobitel.lk/one-shot-ultra.
