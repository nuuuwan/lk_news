Train transporting Prima flour derails near Ragama Station

Ragama, Jan 19 (Daily Mirror) -Â  The Railway Department reported of a disruption in train services after a train transporting Prima flour derailed near the Ragama railway station this afternoon.

The incident occurred as the engine of the train from Colombo, experienced a malfunction, resulting in the detachment of two wheels.

This led to a significant interruption in the train services on the Puttalam line. An official revealed that trains from Colombo to Puttalam are currently experiencing delays at the Ragama station due to the blockage of the upper track on the Puttalam line.
