ComBank commences planting 10,000 trees in Kilinochchi and Jaffna districts

The Commercial Bank of Ceylon has embarked on a project to plant 10,000 trees in the northern districts of Jaffna and Kilinochchi as part of the Bankâ€™s country-wide effort to promote sustainability.
