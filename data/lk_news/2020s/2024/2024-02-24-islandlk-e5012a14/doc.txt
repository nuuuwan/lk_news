Sun Siyam Pasikudah invites locals and residents to experience East coast luxury

Sun Siyam Pasikudah announced an offer for locals and residents in Lanka to experience the captivating beauty and cultural richness of the country’s East Coast, said a press release.

It said: Guests staying a minimum of two nights will be treated to exclusive benefits, including 20% discount on all food & beverage experiences, 10% discount at The Spa treatments, complimentary room upgrades, and early check-ins, late check-outs that are subject to availability. This enticing offer is available until March 31st, 2024, providing an opportunity for guests to seize the moment and experience the best of Pasikudah in unparalleled luxury and style.

The fascinating allure of Pasikudah’s pristine beaches often dubbed as ‘out of this world’ and the vibrant culture of the East, a go-to destination, is well worth a visit. Residents and locals in Sri Lanka can enjoy luxurious accommodations, exquisite dining experiences, and rejuvenating spa treatments, all tailored to offer an experience that lasts a lifetime

Sun Siyam Pasikudah, with its bold monochromatic interiors balanced with rich golden tones, boasts 34 spacious and contemporary one-to-two-bedroom garden and beach pavilions. One is surely in for more than a vacation; it’s a grand adventure, a cultural odyssey, and an oasis of tranquillity. Relish the opulent infinity pool or unwind at the karaoke center. Pamper yourself with wellness treatments, find tranquility with yoga and meditation sessions, or immerse in sports like tennis and badminton. Dive into cultural exploration with local arts and crafts, embark on a serene sailing excursion along Pasikudah’s picturesque coastline or savor a private dining affair beneath the twinkling stars on the property.

Be in for a gastronomic journey, with a variety of distinctive dishes that honour the rich flavours and colonial history of Sri Lanka. Choose from The Cellar, which has the best wine collection on Sri Lanka’s east coast; the Tea House, which offers an immersive tea-tasting experience; Slice & Grill, which offers a classic poolside dining experience; or the Beach Shack, which serves up tropical drinks with a touch of Caribbean flare.

Spend the morning exploring the ancient kingdom of Polonnaruwa by bicycle, or adventure through the Minneriya National Park. Step inside the largest preserved cave complex in Dambulla or climb to the summit of the ancient rock fortress Sigiriya or treat yourself to watch the majestic whales . At the property, spend an evening with our sommelier to sample different wines perfectly paired with gourmet dishes and desserts.
