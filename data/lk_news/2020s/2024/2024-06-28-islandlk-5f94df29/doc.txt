Virtusa’s Vice President lauds SL’s ‘transformative developments’

Roch Ferreira, Vice President and Global Head of Corporate Marketing at Virtusa, emphasized current transformative developments in Sri Lanka during a recent roundtable aimed at enhancing media relationships and presence in the country.

Speaking at roundtable at the Kingsbury, he added: “Receiving the Great Place to Work certification for the second year in a row is a testament to our efforts in creating a nurturing and inclusive workplace. We are proud to offer local talent the chance to work on global projects, providing invaluable experience and career growth.”

Ferreira emphasized the integral role Sri Lanka plays as a talent destination for Virtusa and the company’s interest in engaging local media to disseminate the employer brand messaging in the local market.

Ferreira also briefed journalists on the recent launch of ‘Virtusa Helio’, the company’s Gen-AI offering, the launch of the Virtusa consulting practice and continued commitment to ESG and engineering with purpose.

Highlighting Virtusa’s commitment to local talent and Sri Lanka’s strategic importance as a hub for global projects, Ferreira spoke enthusiastically about the company’s achievements, including its consecutive Great Place to Work certification and the launch of innovative initiatives like ‘Virtusa Helio’ and its consulting practice.

Ferreira praised Virtusa’s role in nurturing Sri Lankan professionals, facilitating their journey from campuses to leadership roles across diverse sectors.

He emphasized the company’s engagement with local media to strengthen its employer brand and support national digitalization efforts. Additionally, he announced Virtusa’s participation in upcoming industry events and its commitment to environmental and social governance (ESG) initiatives.

Virtusa, a global leader in digital engineering and technology services, partners with Forbes Global 2000 companies across various industries. With a focus on its unique “Engineering First” approach, Virtusa collaborates closely with customers to drive innovation and deliver impactful solutions that address their most critical business challenges.
