Russia – Sri Lanka Business Forum and B2B session

In a significant stride towards fostering international business ties, the National Chamber will be conducting a “Russia-Sri Lankan Business Forum” on March 5 from 12.00 noon to 4.30 p.m. at the National Chamber Auditorium, No 450, D R Wijewardena Mawatha, Colombo 10.

The forum will introduce a 11-member business delegation from Russia, headed by Dr. Alexander Rybas, Trade Commissioner of the Russian Federation, to explore trade and investment opportunities between Sri Lanka and Russia.

The visiting delegation represents diverse sectors such as pharmaceuticals, trade, fertilizer, hi-tech, infrastructure development, logistics, shipbuilding and port infrastructure, IT solutions, education and banking & finance.

Prospective attendees could contact the National Chamber Nishanthi / Thushari (0762 555 707 / 0114 741 788) or send a request to nishanthi@nationalchamber.lk; sect@nationalchamber.lk. Allocation of B2B appointments will be on a first-come-first-served basis, due to the limited number of meetings that could be accommodated.
