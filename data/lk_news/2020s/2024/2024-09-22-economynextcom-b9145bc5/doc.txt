Sri Lanka Marxist leader Dissanayaka leaps in postal votes

ECONOMYNEXT – Sri Lanka Marxists leader Anura Kumara Dissanayaka has won in six electoral districts  out of seven in postal votes with huge margins, official election results showed.

Dissanayaka secured wins in Ratnapura, Galle, Hambantota, Monaragala, Trincomalee, and Polnannaruwa electoral district.

Opposition leader Sajith Premadas narrowly won the postal votes in ethnic minority Tamils-dominated Vanni district.

Dissanayaka has topped the list in the postal votes released so far with 92,949 votes, almost 57 percent of the votes so far counted.

Premadasa has secured 32,399 votes or 20% of the total votes, while the current President Ranil Wickremesinghe has secured 30,419 votes or 18.6 percent, the official data showed. (Colombo/September 22/2024)
