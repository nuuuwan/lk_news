‘NSB marks 52nd anniversary with pride’

NSB (The National Savings Bank) commemorated its 52nd anniversary with a meaningful celebration held at the NSB Head Office, graced by the presence of its chairman, Dr. Harasha Cabral PC, Senior Director, Dushantha Basnayake, and GM/CEO, Ms. Shashi Kandambi, senior management and members of Team NSB. The event was a testament to NSB’s rich legacy of service excellence and unwavering commitment to its stakeholders, a NSB press release said.

The release added: ‘The anniversary festivities commenced with solemn religious observances, honoring the diversity and unity of all four religions. The dignitaries present participated in these ceremonies, symbolizing NSB’s respect for cultural pluralism and inclusivity.

‘Following the religious rites, two inspiring speeches were delivered, setting the tone for the day’s proceedings. Chairman of NSB, Dr. Harsha Cabral, expressed his profound belief in the boundless potential of NSB staff members. He emphasized their pivotal role in driving positive change, not only within the organization but also in contributing to the betterment of the nation. Dr. Cabral underscored the symbiotic relationship between NSB’s success and the well-being of its workforce, asserting that as the organization thrives and prospers, so too do its dedicated members.

‘GM/CEO, Ms. Shashi Kandambi echoed Dr. Cabral’s sentiments, highlighting the collective strength of Team NSB in realizing shared goals for the benefit of all stakeholders. Kandambi emphasized the importance of setting ambitious targets and delivering excellence in all endeavors. She envisioned an NSB that is not only technically proficient but also forward-thinking and customer-centric, poised to exceed expectations and delight customers at every turn.’
