New Governors appointed to Southern, North Western Provinces

President Ranil Wickremesinghe yesterday appointed two new Governors for the Southern and North Western Provinces.

Lakshman Yapa Abeywardena took oath as the Governor of the Southern Province before President Ranil Wickremesinghe while Nazeer Ahamed was sworn in as the Governor of the North Western Province. Secretary to the President Saman Ekanayake was present on this occasion.
