Sri Lanka private credit expands in November

ECONOMYNEXT – Sri Lanka’s private credit expanded 62.9 billion rupees to 7,268 billion rupees in November 2023, while credit to government expanded marginally by 30.1 billion rupees, official data show.

Central bank credit declined by 36.2 billion rupees to 2,317.4 billion rupees.

Credit to state enterprises went up by 10.4 billion rupees to 1,120 billion rupees. (Colombo/Jan16/2023)
