Signature partners with Sri Lanka Hockey Federation as ‘Official Formal Wear Partner’ for Junior Men’s Asian Federation Hockey Cup

Signature, Sri Lanka’s most admired menswear brand, partnered with the Sri Lanka Hockey Federation to sponsor the Junior Men’s Asian Federation Hockey (Cup) Tournament as the ‘Official Formal Wear Partner’. The prestigious tournament took place in Singapore from June 13th to June 23rd, bringing together the finest young hockey talents from across Asia.

As the Official Formal Wear Partner for the Junior Men’s Asian Federation Hockey Tournament, Signature outfitted the Sri Lankan team with impeccable formal wear, showcasing the brand’s dedication to excellence and style. This partnership highlighted Signature’s role in the fashion industry and its commitment to supporting and nurturing young talent in the sporting arena, contributing to the development of hockey in Sri Lanka and beyond.

Amjad Hameed, Director of Signature, stated, “We were thrilled to partner with the Sri Lanka Hockey Federation and support the Junior Men’s Asian Federation Hockey Tournament. At Signature, we believe in empowering individuals to achieve their best, whether through fashion or sports. This partnership was a testament to our dedication to excellence and our commitment to making a positive impact in various spheres.”

Established in 1990, Signature has built a reputation for offering exceptional value and style in both formal and casual wear categories. The brand has earned the trust and admiration of Sri Lanka’s fashion-conscious men through its extensive collection, which includes formal, ceremonial, smart casual, casual, linen, party wear, suits, shirts, blazers, and trousers. Signature’s attention to detail and commitment to quality ensure that every piece enhances confidence and style.

In addition to its clothing line, Signature provides a wide range of leather products and men’s accessories, ensuring that every outfit is perfectly complemented with the right finishing touches. This holistic approach to men’s fashion has solidified Signature’s position as the leading menswear brand in Sri Lanka.

With a network of exclusive branches across the island and the convenience of online shopping at https://signature.lk with island-wide delivery, Signature continues to be the go-to fashion destination for men who seek value, style, and confidence.
