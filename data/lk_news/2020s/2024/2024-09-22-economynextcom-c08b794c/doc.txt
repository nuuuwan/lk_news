Sri Lanka lifts post-election curfew at 12 noon Sunday

ECONOMYNEXT – Sri Lanka has lifted an island-wide post-election Police curfew at 12 noon on Sunday (22), local media said quoting Police Spokesman Nihal Talduwa.

The curfew was imposed after voting concluded on what election monitors called the most peaceful election in the island’s history.

Counting of the votes has begun and results are being released throughout at designated counting stations. Marxist JVP leader Anura Kumara Dissanayaka is leading the votes with around 40 percent as of 1155 hours. (Colombo/September 2s/2024)
