Electricity tariff revision proposal presented to the PUCSL

COLOMBO (News 1st) – The Ceylon Electricity Board has presented the electricity tariff revision proposal to the Public Utilities Commission of Sri Lanka.

The PUCSL says that the proposal will be made public as well.

The PUCSL adds that further action will be taken regarding the revision of the electricity tariff, subject to the provisions of the commission.
