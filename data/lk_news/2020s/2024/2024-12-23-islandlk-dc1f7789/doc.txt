NSB triumphs at CMA Excellence in Integrated Reporting Awards 2024

National Savings Bank (NSB) has once again proven its commitment to transparency, accountability, and outstanding corporate reporting by securing two prestigious accolades at the CMA Excellence in Integrated Reporting Awards 2024. The Bank proudly clinched the title of Winner in the State Banks Category and earned recognition as one of the Ten Best Annual Reports for its Integrated Annual Report 2023.

Organized annually by the Institute of Certified Management Accountants of Sri Lanka (CMA), the Excellence in Integrated Reporting Awards is designed to celebrate organizations that adopt and demonstrate international best practices in integrated reporting. These awards focus on assessing an organization’s ability to provide stakeholders with a holistic view of its performance, sustainability efforts, governance, and long-term value creation.

NSB’s recognition at this esteemed platform reflects the Bank’s unwavering dedication to transparency, accuracy, and stakeholder communication. The Integrated Annual Report 2023 stands as a shining example of the Bank’s ability to align financial performance with environmental, social, and governance (ESG) considerations while presenting a comprehensive narrative of its operational resilience, growth strategies, and contributions to national progress.

Being recognized as the Winner in the State Banks Category underscores NSB’s leadership in the public sector banking industry, where it has consistently set new benchmarks for financial and non-financial reporting. In addition, being ranked among the Ten Best Annual Reports is a further testament to the meticulous detail, clarity, and innovation embedded in NSB’s reporting practices.

Over the years, NSB has been continuously recognized for its excellence in annual reporting, reaffirming its position as a leading financial institution committed to the highest standards of corporate governance and transparency. The Bank’s integrated reporting framework focuses on demonstrating value creation for all stakeholders, including customers, investors, employees, and society at large.
