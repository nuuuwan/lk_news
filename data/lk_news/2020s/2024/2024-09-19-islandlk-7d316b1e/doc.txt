Prime Residencies celebrates groundbreaking of ultra-luxury Boutique development

Sri Lanka’s most prolific and trusted property developer, Prime Lands Residencies PLC, celebrated the groundbreaking of its latest ultra-luxury boutique development, ‘The Seasons Colombo 8’. The foundation stone laying ceremony marked the official commencement of construction for this unique living experience, poised to set new standards in luxury living.

The event was graced by Prime Group’s Chairman and Co-Chairperson, along with distinguished guests and stakeholders, underlining this significant milestone. It also heralded the beginning of an exciting journey for a vibrant and special community of the One Collection: the Group’s ultra-luxury residential segment.

With 30 years of experience in the industry, Prime Group has earned the trust of its homeowners by consistently delivering projects of the highest quality and reliability. Backed by this legacy of excellence, the construction of the new project will follow the strictest standards, ensuring superior craftsmanship and attention to detail throughout the development process.

Located in the highly residential area of Colombo 08, between Chandralekha Mawatha and Fairfield Gardens, ‘The Seasons Colombo 8’ is a boutique development offering just 44 residencies, each spanning approximately 2000 square feet in space. Meticulously designed to evoke a sense of grandeur and comfort, this development caters to a niche affluent segment of homebuyers, with many state-of-the-art amenities including separate dedicated storage solutions for every household.

Commenting on the project, Premalal Brahmanage, Chairman of Prime Group, stated, “We have listened to our customers and delivered exactly what they have been asking for—a spacious, elegant living environment.
