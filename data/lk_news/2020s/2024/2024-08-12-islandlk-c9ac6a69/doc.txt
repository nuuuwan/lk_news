Right choice for lobby music…

Roger Menezes is in town, performing at the Galadari Hotel’s Lobby Bar.He moves into action four days a week – on Thursdays, Fridays, Saturdays and Sundays – from 7.00 pm to 11.00 pm.

Roger’s versatility and repertoire blends perfectly with the atmosphere of the Lobby Bar.This talented artiste plays a variety of instruments and his songs are melodious.

He will be at the Galadari till the latter part of this month and then head for home – Sydney, in Australia.Australia has been his home for the past 50 years, he said, but added that he is now keen to return to his homeland, Sri Lanka, and enjoy life here.

Roger mentioned that he would be back to perform at the Galadari, next month, and again in December.In the good old days, Roger performed with many top local outfits, including the Jetliners, Fireflies, Thunderbirds, Sam the Man, Savages and with his own band Roger and The Webs.
