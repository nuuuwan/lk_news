David Pieris Renewable Energy opens head office in Pelawatte

David Pieris Group Chairman and Managing Director Rohana Dissanayake cutting the ribbon to open the new head office

A significant milestone was achieved in Sri Lanka’s sustainable energy landscape and the David Pieris Group with the inauguration of the new head office of David Pieris Renewable Energy Ltd.

Strategically situated at No. 734B, New Parliament Road, Pelawatta, Battaramulla, the new office was opened by David Pieris Group Chairman and Managing Director Rohana Dissanayake along with the board directors of David Pieris Renewable Energy and directors of Group companies. This short and simple ceremony was attended by representatives from the Sustainable Energy Authority, customers, and other internal and external stakeholders.

In August 2022, the David Pieris Group ventured into the solar space and specialised in providing tailor made solar energy solutions under the brand David Pieris Solar Energy. Within just a year of operations and having installed more than 1.5 megawatts worth of solutions including undertaking a 620KW project in Ranna, they have established a strong foundation within the solar energy space.  In 2024, the David Pieris Renewable Energy Ltd., (DPRE) was incorporated to ensure even greater focus and with the opening its new office they are geared to expand their portfolio and accelerate their growth.

“The inauguration of our new office symbolises the next chapter in our journey in the green energy space,” stated David Pieris Renewable Energy Director Naalaka Madugalle. “We will build on the enduring trust and reputation of the David Pieris brand, along with our extensive island-wide network, to ensure customers throughout the island have access to top-tier solar solutions, backed by exceptional customer service and comprehensive after-sales support. Renewable energy is the future, and we are committed to doing our bit for a greener, cleaner, sustainable Sri Lanka for future generations,” Madugalle stressed.

David Pieris Renewable Energy Ltd., currently offer off-grid, hybrid and on-grid roof mounted solar solutions for both domestic and corporate customers. Uncompromising on quality with customer’s safety first, the Company installs products of renown brands such as JA Solar, JinKo, Growatt and extends extensive long term product warranties. In addition, free insurance for the first year, advice on infrastructure and technical needs, (including safety needs), coordination with CEO and LECO and solar loans and instalment payment methods at competitive pricing are also offered to customers.
