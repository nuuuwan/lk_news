Gestetner opens cutting-edge Customer Experience Centre

Gestetner of Ceylon PLC, the leader of office automation solutions providers in Sri Lanka, is pleased to announce the grand opening of its cutting-edge Customer Experience Center located at Union Place, Colombo 02, recently. This state-of-the-art facility has been precisely designed to meet the unique needs of its esteemed customers, serving as a gateway to the introduction of revolutionary products, technology, and innovation.

A. R. Rasiah, the Alternate Director to Chairman of Gestetner of Ceylon PLC, emphasized, “The establishment of the Customer Experience Center is a strategic move in line with our digital transformation strategy, confirming our unwavering commitment to our customers. Through this initiative, we aim to introduce the latest technological products and experiences that enhance lifestyle. Drawing on our 70 years of industry expertise, Gestetner of Ceylon is at the forefront of office automation, offering a diverse range of products and new solutions to enhance reliability and performance. The Customer Experience Center will empower businesses to leverage Gestetner of Ceylon, expediting their own digital transformation journey.”
