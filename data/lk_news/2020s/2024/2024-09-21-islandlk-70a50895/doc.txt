IIHS hosts Coventry University nursing pinning ceremony

IIHS Multiversity recently celebrated a landmark event, the Coventry University Bachelor’s in Nursing (CUBN) Pinning Ceremony on August 15. This occasion marked a significant milestone for aspiring nursing leaders as they formally transitioned from students to healthcare professionals, ready to take on their roles in the dynamic healthcare industry. The ceremony was graced by distinguished guests, highlighting the importance of the event. Dr. Nihal De Silva, Chancellor and Chairman of IIHS Multiversity, Dr. Kithsiri Edirisinghe, CEO, Co-Founder, and Director of International Relations, and Dr. Amal Harsha De Silva, Pro-Vice-Chancellor – International, played key roles in the day’s proceedings. Further adding to the event’s prominence were Renuka Jayatissa, Vice Chancellor of IIHS, and Hamzi Haniff, Education Programme Manager at the British Council Sri Lanka, reflecting the strong international collaborations IIHS maintains.
