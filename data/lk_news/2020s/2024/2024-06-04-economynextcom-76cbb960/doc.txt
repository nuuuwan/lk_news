Sri Lanka’s Litro gas reduces prices

ECONOMYNEXT – Sri Lanka’s state-run Litro Gas said it was cutting liquefied gas (LP Gas) prices with effect from midnight June 5. The price of a 12.5 kg cylinder will be reduced by 150 rupees to 3,790 rupees.

The price of a 5 kg cylinder has been reduced by 60 rupees to 1,522.

A 2.3 kg cylinder has been reduced by 28 rupees to 712 rupees.

“Litro Gas Lanka Company was able to provide relief to the consumers as market management and international market studies were done efficiently,” Litro Chairman Muditha Peiris said in a statement.

The price of LP gas in the international market has not changed much.
