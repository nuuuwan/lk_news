Sri Lanka rupee opens at 303.90/304.00 to the US dollar

ECONOMYNEXT – Sri Lanka’s rupee opened at 303.90/304.00 to the US dollar in the spot forex market on Thursday, weaker from 304.00/20 on Wednesday, dealers said, while bond yields were up.

A bond maturing on 15.12.2026 was quoted at 11.37/40 percent up from 11.30/40 percent.

A bond maturing on 15.09.2027 was quoted at 12.18/22 percent up from 11.90/12.00 percent.

The Colombo Stock Exchange opened down. The All Share Price Index was down 0.01 percent at 11,239. The S&P SL20 was up 0.07 percent at 3,210. (Colombo/Mar21/2024)
