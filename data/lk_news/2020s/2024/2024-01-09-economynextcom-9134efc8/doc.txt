Sri Lanka export investment zones in KKS, Trinco, Iranawila as PPPs

ECONOMYNEXT – Sri Lanka’s cabinet of ministers has approved building five new investment zones as public private partnerships, Minister Bandula Gunawardana said.

The Asian Development Bank will give technical support, he told reporters Tuesday.

Investment zones will be built in the Kankasanthurai cement company and Parantham chemical company premises and in Mankulam in the North.

Other zones were planned in Trincomalee in the East and in Iranawila in the North West.

Investment zones in Bingiriya and Hambantota will be expanded. (Colombo/Jan09/2023)
