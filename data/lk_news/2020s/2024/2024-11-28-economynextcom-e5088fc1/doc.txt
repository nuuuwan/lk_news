Nine ambassadors, high commissioner present credentials to Sri Lanka president

ECONOMYNEXT – Nine new ambassadors and a high commissioner presented credentials to President Anura Kumara Dissanayake on Thursday morning.

Ambassadors of Burkina Faso to Sri Lanka Désiré Boniface Some, of Bosnia and Herzegovina Haris Hrle, of Azerbaijan Elchin Huseynli, of Georgia Vakhtang Jaoshvili, of Belarus Mikhail Kasko, of Armenia Vahagn Afyan, of Spain Juan Antonio March Pujol, of Congo Raymond Serge Balé, and of Guinea Alassane Conte have been appointed.

High commissioner for Kenya to Sri Lanka Munyiri Peter Maina also presented his credentials on Thursday morning.

“Looking forward to strengthening ties with Burkina Faso, Bosnia & Herzegovina, Azerbaijan, Georgia, Belarus, Armenia, Spain, Congo, Guinea, and Kenya,” Dissanayake said.

All appointed foreign delegates are based in New Delhi. (Colombo/Nov29/2024)
