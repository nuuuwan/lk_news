Sri Lanka’s prime minister marks Gandhi Jayanti

ECONOMYNEXT – Sri Lanka’s Prime Minister Harini Amarasuriya marked the 155th birth anniversary of Mahatma Gandhi by garlanding his bust at the PM’s Office in Colombo.

High Commissioner of India to Sri Lanka Santosh Jha was also present.

India gifted the 42 inches-tall bronze bust of Gandhi in meditative pose in 2019, to commemorate his 150th birth anniversary.

A Gandhi oration by former High Commissioner of Sri Lanka to India Austin Fernando was also organized by the Sri Lanka India Society (SLIS) with the Swami Vivekananda Cultural Centre.

The annual event serves as a platform for reflecting on Gandhi’s teachings and their relevance in today’s world, a statement by the High Commission of India, Colombo said. (Colombo/Oce3/2024)
