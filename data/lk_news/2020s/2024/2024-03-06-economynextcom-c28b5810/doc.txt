Sri Lanka rupee opens stronger at 307.70/90 to US dollar

ECONOMYNEXT – Sri Lanka’s rupee opened stronger at 307.70/90 to the US dollar in the spot forex market Wednesday stronger from 307.95/108.05 to the dollar a day earlier, dealers said.

Bond yields were flat ahead of a bill auction later on Wednesday.
