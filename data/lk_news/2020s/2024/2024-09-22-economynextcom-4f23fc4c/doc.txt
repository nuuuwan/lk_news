Sri Lanka extends post-election curfew by six hours till 12 noon Sunday

ECONOMYNEXT – Sri Lanka has extended a post-election island-wide Police curfew by six hours till 12 noon on Sunday (22), local media said quoting Public Security Minister Tiran Alles .

The curfew was imposed after voting concluded on what election monitors called the most peaceful election in the island’s history.

Counting of the votes has begun and results are being released throughout at designated counting stations. (Colombo/September 2s/2024)
