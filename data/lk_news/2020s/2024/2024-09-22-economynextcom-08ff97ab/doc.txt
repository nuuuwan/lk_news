Sri Lanka Marxist leader Dissanayaka leads in postal votes; Wickremesinghe falls behind

ECONOMYNEXT – Sri Lanka Marxists leader Anura Kumara Dissanayaka has won in 10 electoral districts out of 11 in postal votes with huge margins in the presidential polls held on Saturday, official election results showed.

Incumbent President Ranil Wickremesinghe has secured second highest votes, but almost a third of Dissanayaka’s vote so far.

Dissanayaka secured wins in Ratnapura, Galle, Hambantota, Monaragala, Trincomalee, Polnannaruwa, Matara, Nuwara Eliya, Matale, and Colombo electoral districts.

Opposition leader Sajith Premadas narrowly won the postal votes in ethnic minority Tamils-dominated Vanni district.

Dissanayaka has topped the list in the postal votes released so far with 154,657 votes, 57.8 percent of the votes so far counted.

Wickremesinghe has secured 52,482  votes or 19.6% of the total votes, while Opposition Leader Sajith Premadasa has secured 48,970 votes or 18.3 percent, the official data showed.

The winner has to secure more than 50 percent votes. If fails, he should secure the highest votes after the preferential vote counting from all the candidates except the top two. (Colombo/September 22/2024)
