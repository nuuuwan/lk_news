SLT-MOBITEL launches tailored suite solutions for MSMEs to ‘Soar Beyond Limits’

SLT-MOBITEL has unleashed its new SLT-MOBITEL Enterprise initiative titled ‘Soar Beyond Limits’ presenting Micro, Small, And Medium-Sized Enterprises (MSMEs) a tailored suite of business solutions to achieve digital transformation and increase their contribution towards the economic development of the nation. Through this initiative, SLT-MOBITEL looks forward to enabling MSMEs to drive substantial progress across key metrics including economic growth, technological readiness, and the ability to harness technology for innovation to meet local demands.

At an Enterprise product Forum held recently, SLT-MOBITEL Enterprise provided MSME business owners launched Cubkit web builder, Akaza HR, Akaza ERP and provided participants with the opportunity to immerse and interact with these products.

In addition, the above, SLT-MOBITEL’s tailored suite of products for MSMEs include cloud, security, and connectivity solutions. Cloud offerings comprise Enterprise Premium Cloud, Google Workspace, Eazy Storage, HOSTINGCUB,

CUBKIT – Web Builder, and CGaaS AI Platform. Security solutions feature Windows Defender and Kaspersky Endpoint Security solutions, and connectivity products include FTTH Flash, One Shot Ultra, Secured Business Internet Line (BIL), AI SecureNet, Net Guard, and Net Guard plus.

The solutions offered by SLT-MOBITEL will enable MSMEs to drive customers’ business automation and digitalization. SLT-MOBITEL is actively supporting these organizations to facilitate the integration of innovative technologies into their operations, fostering efficiency, and ensuring long-term sustainability.

By providing digital solutions tailored for Sri Lankan MSMEs, the campaign can equip these businesses with technologies to drive productivity, unlock new opportunities, and build resilience, especially amidst the current economic challenges. Thus, thriving MSMEs can contribute to national growth and employment.
