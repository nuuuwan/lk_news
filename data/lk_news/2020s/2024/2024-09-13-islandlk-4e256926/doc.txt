ASUS expands its lineup with next-gen AI laptops for students

ASUS, the Taiwanese tech giant, today announced the expansion of its laptop portfolio with two new models across Consumer lineups. The AMD AI 300 Series-powered Vivobook S 14 M5406 and your next Copilot+ PC, Vivobook S 15 S5507, with the Qualcomm Snapdragon X Plus processor. Both new laptops redefine user experience by integrating cutting-edge AI capabilities & an innovative design. The products will be accessible at the ASUS Exclusive Stores & selected multi-brand stores across Sri Lanka. The Vivobook S 14 M5406 starts from LKR 539,990, and the Vivobook S 15 S5507 will starts from LKR 479,990.

ASUS’s latest devices are powered by the latest AMD Ryzen™ AI 300 Series & Qualcomm’s Snapdragon X Plus processor.

About the Launch, Sam Huang, Business Development Manager, ASUS Sri Lanka, said, “At ASUS, our mission is to empower users with technology that makes a meaningful impact. Our latest launches – Vivobook S 14 M5406 & Vivobook S 15 S5507 from our flagship series – deliver AI-driven performance in exceptionally designed laptops that meet a wide array of needs along with the latest Microsoft Copilot+ tool. We’re proud to say that our new products are engineered to Simplify productivity, creativity, accessibility & much more”

The Vivobook S 14 OLED M5406 measures up to just 1.39 cm thin and weighs only 1.3 kg. It is equipped with the AMD Ryzen™ AI 9 HX 365 processor and a 50 TOPS XDNA2 NPU, delivering advanced AI capabilities. Efficient multitasking is supported by 24 GB of LPDDR5X RAM and a 1TB M.2 NVMe™ PCIe® 4.0 SSD. The 14-inch ASUS Lumina OLED display provides vivid and accurate visuals. Designed for extended use, the laptop offers 75 WHr Battery, fast Type-C charging, and a range of I/O ports. It has an ASUS ErgoSense keyboard with single-zone RGB backlighting.

The new ASUS Copilot+ PC, Vivobook S 15 S5507 features the latest Qualcomm Snapdragon X Plus processor with integrated Adreno graphics and Qualcomm Hexagon NPU delivering up to 45 TOPS of AI performance. It boasts 16 GB LPDDR5X RAM and a blazing-fast 1 TB PCIe Gen 4.0 SSD. Ultra-thin at just 1.47 cm & lightweight at 1.42 kg, it is equipped with an ergonomic keyboard with single-zone RGB backlighting. Stunning 15.6-inch 3K ASUS Lumina OLED panel, offering a 120 Hz refresh rate for smooth viewing.
