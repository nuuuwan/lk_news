Atlas empowers pre-school teachers with skills to unleash the ‘genius’ in every child

Atlas, Sri Lanka’s leading learning brand, in collaboration with its educational toy range, Atlas PlayPalz, has successfully conducted its 45th training programme across the island and continues to empower the country’s pre–school teacher community through ongoing play-based learning initiatives aimed at enhancing early childhood education. Since its launch in December 2024, this initiative has empowered over 3,500 pre-school teachers, reinforcing Atlas’s commitment to making learning fun and Atlas PlayPalz’s vision of unleashing the genius in every child in Sri Lanka through play.

Atlas PlayPalz’s training programmes are held continuously across all regions of Sri Lanka, consistently empowering pre-school teachers with the knowledge and practical skills needed to implement play-based learning.

Commenting on the success of the programme, Asitha Samaraweera, Managing Director of Atlas Axillia Co. Pvt Ltd, stated, “We truly believe that every child has a spark of genius waiting to be discovered and through these teacher training sessions, we’re not just sharing knowledge, we’re giving educators the tools and confidence to unlock their potential and make learning a joyful, exciting journey for every child.”
