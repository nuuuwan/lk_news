First Capital to host Mid-Year Outlook Investor Forum 2025

First Capital Holdings PLC, a subsidiary of JXG (Janashakthi Group) and a pioneering full-service investment institution, has announced the First Capital Mid-Year Outlook Investor Forum 2025, a virtual event designed to provide investors and stakeholders with an in-depth analysis of Sri Lanka’s economic and investment prospects for the remainder of the year.

Scheduled for 17 July, from 4 p.m. to 5:30 p.m., the forum will explore critical themes including the macroeconomic landscape, fixed income markets, and stock market trends. This year, for the first time, the event will be delivered in Sinhala, Tamil, and English, reinforcing First Capital’s commitment to inclusivity and accessibility across diverse investor groups.

The forum will feature First Capital Research’s carefully crafted outlook on key indicators such as interest rates, the All-Share Price Index (ASPI), and exchange rates — insights that have historically guided investors toward well-informed decisions. This presentation will be delivered by First Capital Holdings PLC Assistant Vice President – Research Ranjan Ranatunga.

An expert panel discussion will bring valuable perspectives from across industries, featuring Access Engineering Executive Vice Chairman Christopher Joshua; Hemas Holdings PLC Group CFO Moiz Rehmanjee; Advantis Bunkering Director and CEO Kushan De Silva; and First Capital Holdings PLC Chief Research and Strategy Officer Dimantha Mathew. The session will be moderated by First Capital Senior Analyst – Research Akna Tennekoon. Together, they will share on-the-ground views from the consumer, construction, and logistics sectors, helping investors better understand sector-specific dynamics within the broader market context.

The forum will be streamed live via Zoom, Facebook Live, and YouTube to ensure wide participation. Interested parties are encouraged to register early to secure their virtual seat.
