Sri Lanka’s Ambeon Capital buys 6.8-pct of Ceylon Hotels Corporation

ECONOMYNEXT- Sri Lanka’s Ambeon Capital said it had acquired a 6.8 percent stake of the ordinary voting shares of Ceylon Hotels Corporation PLC for 320 million rupees.

Ambeon Holdings paid 26 rupees per share, the company said in a stock exchange filing.

Ceylon Hotels Corporation stock was trading down 60 cents at 32 rupees. (Colombo/Aug22/2025)
