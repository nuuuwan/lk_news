Australia U19 Women’s Team In Sri Lanka For T20 & ODI

COLOMBO (News 1st); The Australia Under-19 Women’s Cricket Team has arrived in Sri Lanka for an international tour, set to feature a series of matches against the Sri Lankan U19 Women’s side.The tour includes a total of six matches, comprising five T20 Internationals and one One-Day International (ODI). All matches will be played at the Dambulla International Cricket Stadium.The T20 matches are scheduled to take place on September 19, 20, 23, 25, and 27, while the ODI will be held on September 29.
