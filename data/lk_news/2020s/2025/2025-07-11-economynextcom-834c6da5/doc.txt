Sri Lanka rupee closes weaker, bonds steady

ECONOMYNEXT – Sri Lanka’s rupee closed at 300.80/301.00 US dollar Friday in the spot market down from 300.45/55 Wednesday dealers said, while bond yields were broadly steady, dealers said.

Out of an auction of 200 billion rupees, only 142 billion was subscribed.

In the secondary market a bond maturing 15.12.2026 closed at 8.05/15 percent..

A bond maturing on 15.10.2028 closed at 8.90/9.00 percent down from at 8.92/9.00 on Wednesday.

A bond maturing on 15.12.2029 closed at 9.48/52 percent, unchanged from 9.48/52 percent.
