Sri Lanka’s Seylan Bank Rs15bn debenture gets Rs23bn in subscriptions

ECONOMYNEXT – Sri Lanka’s Seylan Bank Plc’s debenture issue to raise up to 15 billion rupees was oversubscribed, according to a stock exchange filing.

The bank had received 158 applications totaling 23,882,010,000, it said.

The rupee-denominated, Basel III-compliant subordinated debentures were offered at 100 rupees each.
