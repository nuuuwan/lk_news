National Youth Conference to be Held Today After 32-Year Gap

COLOMBO (News 1st): The National Youth Conference is being held this morning under the patronage of President Anura Kumara Dissanayake, marking its return after 32 years.Chairman of the National Youth Services Council, Attorney-at-Law Supun Wijerathna says that the event will take place at the Sugathadasa Indoor Stadium, with the participation of over 6,000 youth representatives from across the island.In the lead-up to the national event, youth clubs were established at the regional level as part of a broader initiative to form a nationwide youth network.
