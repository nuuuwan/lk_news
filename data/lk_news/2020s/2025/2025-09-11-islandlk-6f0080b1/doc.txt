Mercantile Investments partners with IDEAL Motors to offer exclusive leasing solutions for Bolero City Pickup

Mercantile Investments (MI), one of Sri Lanka’s leading financial service providers, has entered into a strategic partnership with IDEAL Motors to offer attractive leasing solutions for the Mahindra Bolero City Pickup, a robust commercial vehicle designed to support the growth of small businesses, traders, and logistics operators.

Through this partnership, Mercantile Investments will keep a dedicated inventory of Bolero City Pickups, guaranteeing customers can get vehicles quickly and easily while also taking advantage of a number of special advantages. These include free first-year insurance, free registration, hospitalization coverage for the driver, up to Rs. 300,000 in accident coverage, and a waiver of rental fees if repairs take more than a month.

This demonstrates Mercantile Investments’ dedication to supporting delivery operators, small business owners, and entrepreneurs by offering reliable, ready-to-drive commercial vehicles together with easy financial solutions. For those involved in trading, logistics, and other commercial ventures, the Bolero City Pickup is the perfect transportation solution due to its reputation for durability and efficiency.

The two organizations’ Memorandum of Understanding (MOU) signing serves to further solidify the longstanding dependability and trustworthiness of Mercantile Investments and IDEAL Motors. Laksanda Gunawardena, Chief Operating Officer of Mercantile Investments, and Ms. Nimisha Welgama, Director of Legal & Corporate Affairs of IDEAL Motors, officially signed the MOU.
