Sri Lanka’s Almas ups stake in Galle Face Capital Partners to over 10-pct

ECONOMYNEXT – Sri Lanka’a Almas Holdings (Pvt) Ltd said it has increased its share in listed investment trust Galle Face Capital Partners to over 10 percent of the company’s issued share capital.

Almas bought 1,350,000 shares at 69 rupees, 159 shares at 72.90 rupees, and 8,722 shares at 73 rupees on August 7, according to a stock exchange filing.

Galle Face Capital Partners stock was trading up at 73.40 rupees noon Monday. (Colombo/Aug11/2025)
