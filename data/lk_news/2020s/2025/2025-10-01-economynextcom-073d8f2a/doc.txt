Sri Lanka president meets Japanese emperor

ECONOMYNEXT – Sri Lanka’s president Anura Disanayake has met Japan’s Emperor Naruhito at the Imperial Palace, his media division said.

“It was a great honour to be received at the Imperial Palace yesterday (30) for a warm and cordial discussion with His Majesty Emperor Naruhito of Japan,” Disanayake said on social media.

“A deeply valued moment that underscores the longstanding friendship between our two nations.”

The two leaders engaged in “a cordial discussion”, the PMD said.

Minister of Foreign Affairs Vijitha Herath was also present. (Colombo/Oct1/2025)
