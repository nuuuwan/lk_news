Sri Lanka’s Millennium Housing Developers trading suspension deferred

ECONOMYNEXT – Trading in Sri Lanka’s Millennium Housing Developers shares will not be suspended as of September 1, according to a stock exchange announcement.

“Since the company has requested a deferment from the SEC in terms of  Rule 7.5(d)(ii)A.12 of the CSE Listing Rules, the trading of the said security will not be suspended until further notice.”

The company faced the trading suspension for non-compliance due to “matter ongoing concern in the Independent Auditor’s Report in the Audited Financial Statements for the year ended 31st March 2023- Rule 7.5.”

The stock was trading flat 20 cents at 7.40 rupees. (Colombo/Sep1/2025)
