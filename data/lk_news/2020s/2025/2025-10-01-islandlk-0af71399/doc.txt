Automobile Association attends AUTOFORGE ‘25

The Automobile Association of Royal College conducted AUTOFORGE’ 25, an exhibition, on September 30, at the Royal College MAS Arena from 8.00am to 6.00pm.

This is a phase II Interactive exhibition of automotive excellence. This event featured cutting edge engineering innovations, pioneering designs and inspiring insights into the future of mobility. Several stakeholders displayed their products and merchandise Items at the exhibition.

It was a colourful and educative exhibition, especially for the upcoming younger generation.

Athula Wijewardana – Principal of Royal College graced the occasion.

Automobile Association of Ceylon (AAC) was represented by Prasanna De Zoysa – Member of the EXCO & Sectional Chairman Road Safety & Environmental Management and Devapriya Hettiarachchi – Secretary. They were Guests of Honour at the event.
