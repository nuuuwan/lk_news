Tree falls on bus in Olcott Mawatha

Colombo, Sept. 26 (Daily Mirror) - A tree fell on to a bus travelling along Olcott Mawatha in Colombo this morning, near the Railway Headquarters, causing traffic congestion in the area.

Police said no injuries were reported in the incident. The passengers and driver of the bus escaped unharmed.

However, the fallen tree has obstructed part of the roadway, resulting in vehicles being limited to a single lane.
