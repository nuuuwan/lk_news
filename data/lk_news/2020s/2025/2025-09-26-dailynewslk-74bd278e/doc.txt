British School celebrates  Under-12 Girls’ football success

The British School in Colombo clinched third place in the Girls’ Under-12 category at the International Schools Under-12 Football Tournament, played last week at the Moragasmulla Football Grounds and organised by the British School.

The tournament featured both boys’ and girls’ teams from several leading international schools. The event also gave young players valuable exposure to structured football in a professional setting.

For the British School, the bronze medal was a proud moment, showing the growth of their football programme and the dedication of the coaching staff. (SJ)
